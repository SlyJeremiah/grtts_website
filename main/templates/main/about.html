About GRTTS

Who We Are

GRTTS is a ranger training, deployment and management private company. We have trained over 500 rangers to date and we cover nearly 45,000ha of private and community owned wildlife areas across Zimbabwe.

Our History

Founded by Zimbabwean conservationists and wildlife managers, GRTTS was registered in Zimbabwe in 2017 and has been active in wildlife protection since then.

Our Mission

To empower men and women to serve on the frontlines of wildlife and environmental protection through professional training and deployment.
