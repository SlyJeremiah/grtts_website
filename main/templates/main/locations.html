About Our Locations

We have our main training centres in Gweru and Victoria Falls. We also deploy our expert Sergeants and Corporals on site in different wildlife properties to manage and assist supervising existing scouts and rangers on the ground.

Our deployment locations span across private and community-owned wildlife areas, covering nearly 45,000 hectares of protected land.
